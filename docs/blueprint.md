# **App Name**: Pedro's Pulse

## Core Features:

- Immersive Hero Section: Prominent display of 'Pedro Luigi' and a dedicated, styled space for the portfolio video as the main focal point, capturing the initial attention with bold artistic flair.
- Artistic Video Player: Integrate a portfolio video player supporting YouTube/Vimeo links or local uploads, featuring customizable 'zine/collage/ripped' stylized borders, hover effects, and/or animated frames for an immersive look.
- AI Aesthetic Enhancer Tool: An AI-powered tool to suggest and apply artistic filters and overlays (e.g., glitch, granular textures, zine-inspired collages) to uploaded project visuals, enabling Pedro Luigi to maintain a consistent and unique experimental aesthetic.
- Dynamic Projects Showcase: A 'Projects' section featuring dynamic layouts, bold imagery, and dramatic entry effects for each project, embodying the requested artistic and experimental visual style.
- About Me Section: A creative 'About' section allowing Pedro Luigi to introduce himself, incorporating multimedia elements and expressive typography that align with the overall artistic direction.
- Interactive Contact Form: A 'Contact' section with an unconventional form design and engaging microinteractions to encourage users to connect, while respecting the aesthetic guidelines.
- Responsive & Immersive UI: A mobile-first responsive layout with dramatic page transitions, fluid animations, impactful entry effects, mouse hover microinteractions, and cinematic-feel scrolling to deliver a truly immersive experience across devices.

## Style Guidelines:

- The visual design embraces a dark scheme, providing a dramatic backdrop for the vibrant color palette. This choice highlights the experimental nature and strong contrasts, making the vivid tones pop. The use of a deep magenta for the background hue subtly connects it to the primary color, ensuring thematic consistency while allowing other bold colors to stand out.
- Primary Color: A vibrant magenta-pink (#EC1DBC) reflecting digital art and neon aesthetics. It is bold and highly saturated to stand out on a dark background, evoking energy and creativity without being overly conventional. (HSL: 315, 90%, 60%)
- Background Color: A very dark, desaturated purplish-grey (#31262F) with the same magenta hue as the primary color. This choice creates a sophisticated and deep foundation for the vibrant foreground elements, enhancing their luminosity. (HSL: 315, 20%, 15%)
- Accent Color: A deep, electric violet (#870DCA) that provides a strong contrast to the primary magenta and the dark background. Its distinct hue and brightness add visual interest and help delineate interactive elements, fitting the bold and experimental criteria. (HSL: 285, 80%, 40%)
- Body and headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for an audacious, expressive, and subtly 'imperfect' typographic presence, echoing the digital, experimental, and zine-inspired aesthetic throughout the site.
- Use deconstructed, glitch-art inspired, or subtly 'damaged' icon sets that align with the experimental and underground aesthetic. Icons should complement the collage-like and grainy textures of the UI.
- The layout prioritizes asymmetry and dynamic composition, resembling digital collages rather than traditional grid structures. Elements will feature deliberate overlaps, textured 'tears,' and an 'imperfection' that enhances the zine-inspired aesthetic. Emphasis is on mobile-first design with fluid adaptations for larger screens.
- Implement dramatic transitions between sections, cinematic-style scroll effects (subtle parallax), impactful element entry animations (fade, zoom with glitch effects), and fluid microinteractions on hover to create a memorable and immersive user experience. Animations should feel dynamic and contribute to the overall experimental artistic flow.
